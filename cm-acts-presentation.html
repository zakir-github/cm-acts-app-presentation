The gap it closes

Concerns raised in a community rarely travel in a straight line to the office that can act on them.

CM‑ACTS gives residents across BARMM a direct, trackable route from a reported concern to a resolved one — replacing word-of-mouth and lost paperwork with a single accountable record.

Direct
Route from resident to responsible office
Tracked
Every concern keeps a visible status
What the app does

Three moves, one accountable loop.

01 — Speak up

Report a concern

Residents log an issue directly from their phone — no office visit, no lost paper trail.

02 — Follow it

Track the status

Each concern carries a visible status, so the person who raised it can see it move.

03 — See it through

Confirm resolution

Concerns close with a record of what was done, building a public account of follow-through.

How it works

From a concern in a community to a closed case.

01

Submit

A resident describes the concern within the app — the entry point for the whole system.

02

Route

The concern reaches the office responsible for that kind of issue in BARMM.

03

Act

The responsible office takes action and updates the status the resident sees.

04

Resolve

The case closes with a record — the concern is monitored end to end, not just filed.

01 — Architecture

MVVM with Clean Architecture

Clear separation between data, domain, and presentation layers across the :app and :common modules.

02 — Navigation

Driven by main_nav.xml

Handled via an activityViewModel inside fragments to trigger transitions between MAIN, DETAIL, and CATEGORY.

03 — Data flow

Remote, local, and shared state

  • Remote: Retrofit with Moshi fetches feedback and notifications.
  • Local: Room (UserDao) persists user session data.
  • State: A shared State<T> (from :common) drives loading spinners and error toasts consistently.
04 — UI components

Custom toolbars & adapters

Custom toolbars included via appBarInclude across most layouts; the messaging thread leans on ConcatAdapter to combine sender info with reply lists.

01 — Architecture

MVVM + Coordinator pattern

Screens separated into Controllers, View Models, Coordinators, and Services — clean single-responsibility throughout the Cleanup/Screens/ directory.

02 — Networking

Moya + Realm offline cache

Moya handles API requests with AccessTokenAuthorizable for auth; Realm persists user data, feedback state, and agency lists locally. Network logging enabled for debugging.

03 — Notifications

Unread badge + preferences + pagination

Full notification feature: paginated list, mark-as-read, unread/all filtering, badge with a dedicated unread endpoint, and a notification preferences screen.

04 — Key integrations

DropDown, ImagePreview, attachments

  • DropDown: searchable dropdown for agency selection.
  • ImagePreview: media preview for feedback attachments.
  • File validation: 3-file cap, 8 MB total limit.
  • CLLocationManager: iOS 14-compatible location auth.
